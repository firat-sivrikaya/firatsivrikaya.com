About Me

I was born in Ankara in 1997. I finished my secondary education at Ankara Ataturk Anatolian Highschool (AAAL) in 2014. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and graduated from Bilkent University, Ankara. I have spent one semester as an exchange student at Roskilde University, Denmark.

During my university years, I have been into various activities. I have founded a student club, Bilkent eSports Society and remained as the chairman for 3 years. I have organized Turkey's first eSports conference Bilkent eSports Weekend in 2016.

My first internship was in my sophomore year, at Thunderbolt Studios, Beverly Hills, CA, as an software engineer intern. I contributed to the development and optimization of customer websites and internal websites. In my junior year, I did my internship at Turkish Aerospace Industries, Inc. (TAI) as a software engineer intern. At TAI, I worked at Helicopters Division, Flight Mechanics & Autopilot Systems department. For the department's usage, I developed FSUS (FlightLab Simulink Utility Software), which aims to optimize the time spent for modeling and simulation operations by using a multi-client server architecture.

Currently I am living in Berlin and working as a Junior Backend Engineer at Talentwunder GmbH. Talentwunder is an award-winning HR-Tech company which helps companies hire the best talents worldwide. It has a specialized search engine that filters out over 2 billion profiles from more than 70 social platform and performs big-data analysis to calculate various metrics for every candidate, such as change probability, and willingness to relocate.

As leisure time activities, I love streaming on Twitch, playing electric guitar and travelling. I have travelled over 40 cities in Europe & United States. I listen lots of genres, but my favorites are Metal and Electronic. I do have a song on Spotify that I produced back in my sophomore year. Stack Overflow Viewer Application

2017

Stack Overflow Viewer Application (SOVA) is a single-page application that allows users browse posts in Stack Overflow, display various stats and mark the posts that they want to save for later. The application is based on ASP.NET and runs its own API instead of directly accessing StackExchange API. To avoid excessive storage usage, the database includes a small portion of the posts (~100.000).

Technologies used: Bootstrap, KnockoutJS, LINQ, Entity Framework, ASP.Net, C#, MySQL

MIND CONTROL PLATFORMER

2017

Mind control platformer is a Unity-powered 3D-looking-2D interactive platformer game. Different that the other platformer games, player can perform actions in the game with his brain signals that are read by the EMOTIV EPOC headset. Due to course restrictions, we had to strictly follow Model-View-Controller and made the game object-oriented in Unity (which is not the popular way).

Technologies used: C#, Unity, Emotiv EPOC
